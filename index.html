Portfolio

Our final project consisted of building a portfolio to house our best work. This was not limited to the class, it simply had to be a collection of whatever you felt best described your talents. We first created wireframes (Wireframe 1↗, Wireframe 2↗, Wireframe 3↗). Then, we made styleboards (Styleboard 1↗ and Styleboard 2↗) to showed the basic design of the site. For my portfolio I decided to include the four talents that resonate most with me: Computer science, digital arts, writing, and innovation.

Projects

Resume

Here, I've designed a simple, one page layout of my resume. The goal was to make it clean and easy to use, specifically for potential employers. I used only two colors for the entire site, and switched them before and after the anchor scroll to change the design look, but keep the style.

Recipe Book

This website was to showcase a certain set of recipes that may hold some importance to you. I chose to go with the theme of "What Can I Do With...?" In order to give users a way to discard foods that they want to use up. We first created a wireframe↗, one styleboard↗, and then a second styleboard↗ to ensure we covered a wide range of styles.

Heroes

Heroes was built to showcase the people that I admire. Everyone included in this website have shaped my view on the world in one way or another and have truly inspired me to live a better life. This project started off with a wireframe↗, followed by one styleboard↗, a second styleboard↗, and a third styleboard↗.

Definitions

For this project we were given a word to build a website upon. It needed to be only one page, however the goal was to make the website have some functionality that would represent the word. This could be through color or animation

How-To

For my How-To project, I did a walk-through of how to write the word "happy" in my language of Hindi. I wanted to make it almost like a lesson, where users could easily access a menu containing the steps in case they needed to go back to a step.

Poem 1.0

This was the main, in-depth version of the poem project we had been working on, We had to plan out the website first on paper, including the creativity we had before but still adding onto the charm.
